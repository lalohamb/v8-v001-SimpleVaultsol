### **Short Description** (50 words)
SENTINEL is AI agent infrastructure for Cronos that enables dApps to monitor, predict, and optimize autonomously. Our platform includes a production-ready vault with 4 AI agents, plus a reusable SDK for developers. Every decision is transparent, safe, and logged on-chain.

### **What problem does this solve?**
DeFi applications need intelligent automation but current solutions sacrifice either safety or transparency. Manual monitoring doesn't scale, black-box AI isn't trustworthy, and unsafe automation is dangerous. SENTINEL provides AI-powered decision-making with deterministic safety guarantees and complete on-chain transparency.

### **What did you build?**
Two products: (1) SENTINEL Platform - a complete vault management system with 4 AI agents, deployable to Cronos mainnet, (2) SENTINEL SDK - a protocol-agnostic NPM package that enables any developer to add AI agents to their Cronos contracts. Both include our novel clamping layer for safety.

### **What makes it innovative?**
Our clamping layer is a deterministic safety envelope that prevents AI from making dangerous recommendations while preserving intelligence. Combined with hybrid AI/deterministic modes, advisory-only recommendations, and 100% on-chain transparency, we've solved the fundamental challenge of AI in DeFi: how to be intelligent without being unsafe.

### **How does it use Cronos?**
Native Cronos EVM integration via ethers.js, deployable smart contracts on Cronos mainnet, real-time event monitoring of Cronos transactions, and ecosystem-first design with SDK that works with any Cronos contract. We're making Cronos the first AI-native blockchain.

### **What's next?**
Publish SDK to NPM, deploy platform to production, partner with 3-5 Cronos dApps for integration, complete the 12-agent catalog, implement advanced policy features, and expand to enterprise offerings with control plane and support packages.
